## Who We Are

GetMediaTools builds practical browser-based media tools focused on speed, clarity, and accessibility.

## Our Mission

We aim to make common media tasks simple for everyone with:

- Fast tools that work across devices.
- Clean interfaces that reduce friction.
- Practical, transparent policies.

## Product Principles

- Keep the product useful without unnecessary complexity.
- Prioritize reliability and abuse prevention.
- Respect user privacy and provide clear documentation.
