## Overview

GetMediaTools is built to minimize personal data collection. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can contact us about your data.

## Information We Collect

- Technical request data required to operate our services (for example, request metadata, abuse-prevention logs, and error diagnostics).
- Contact form data that you submit voluntarily (name, email, reason, message).
- Analytics events used to improve performance and product quality.

## How We Use Information

- Provide and secure our tools.
- Prevent abuse, fraud, and service degradation.
- Respond to support requests.
- Improve reliability, UX, and feature quality.

## Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for operations, security, legal obligations, and support. We aim to limit retention and remove unnecessary records promptly.

## Third-Party Services

GetMediaTools may use third-party infrastructure providers (hosting, analytics, email delivery, anti-bot verification). These providers process data under their own policies and contractual safeguards.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing.

## Contact

For privacy requests, use the [Contact Us](../contact-us/) page.
