## Overview

This refund policy applies to paid products or credit packages offered by GetMediaTools.

## Eligibility

Refund requests may be approved when:

- Billing errors occurred.
- Service was unavailable for an extended period due to verified platform issues.
- The purchased feature could not be delivered as described.

## Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are generally not issued for:

- Abuse, policy violations, or account restrictions.
- Requests outside the refund window.
- Issues caused by third-party platform changes outside our control.

## Refund Window

Please submit your request within 7 days of purchase unless local consumer law requires a different timeline.

## How to Request a Refund

Use the [Contact Us](../contact-us/) page and include your transaction details so we can review quickly.

## Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days depending on your payment provider.
