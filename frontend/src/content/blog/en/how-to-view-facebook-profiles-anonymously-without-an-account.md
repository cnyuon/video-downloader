## The Fundamentals of Facebook Profile Tracking

### Can Someone Tell If You're Looking at Their Facebook Page?

Let’s definitively answer the core anxiety driving your search: can someone tell if you're looking at their facebook page? The answer is no. Facebook does not natively notify users when someone views their profile, nor do they provide a hidden dashboard showing profile visitors. 

The persistent myth that users can track views stems from a misunderstanding of the "People You May Know" algorithm. This algorithm suggests friends based on background data—such as shared contacts, overlapping location data, or linked phone numbers—not direct profile views. Your browsing activity remains hidden from the profile owner.
