## Bypassing the App Prompt: URL Manipulation & Desktop Tricks

### How to Stop Mobile Browser Redirects (Expertise)

TikTok's mobile website uses aggressive deep-linking scripts designed to force you into the App Store or Google Play Store. To stop these mobile browser redirects, you need to manipulate the URL parameters before the page fully loads.

When you receive a shortened link (e.g., `vm.tiktok.com/XYZ`), do not click it directly. Instead:
1. Copy the link and paste it into an incognito or private mobile browser tab.
2. Let the URL expand to its full length, but hit the "Stop Loading" (X) button before the pop-up script executes.
3. Look at the end of the URL. Manually append the parameters `?is_from_webapp=1&sender_device=pc` to the end of the link and hit enter. 

This URL manipulation tricks TikTok's servers into thinking the request is coming from a desktop environment, effectively disabling the forced app redirect script.

## Can I View TikTok as a Guest? (Official Methods)

### How Do You Become a Guest on TikTok? (Experience)

Many users ask, "can I view TikTok as a guest?" The answer is yes, through TikTok's official web-based Guest Mode. 

So, how do you become a guest on TikTok? 
1. Open a desktop browser (or a mobile browser in Desktop Mode) and navigate to `tiktok.com`.
2. A login prompt will eventually appear in the center of the screen. 
3. Look for the **"Continue as Guest"** option at the bottom of the prompt. If that option is geo-restricted in your region, simply click the **"X"** in the top right corner of the login modal.
4. You are now browsing in Guest Mode. TikTok will assign you a temporary session ID to track your immediate scrolling behavior for the "For You" page.

### The Strict Limitations of Guest Browsing

While Guest Mode allows you to consume content, it is heavily restricted. Because you do not have a persistent profile, you **cannot**:
* Like, comment on, or share videos internally.
* Follow creators or build a persistent "Following" feed.
* Upload or post your own content.
* View live streams or age-restricted content (as TikTok cannot verify your age).
* Save your watch history once the browser session is closed.
