**Can someone tell if you screenshot their Instagram profile?**
No. Instagram does not track or notify users when someone takes a screenshot of their main profile, grid posts, follower lists, or bio. You can capture this public-facing information completely anonymously.

**Does Instagram notify when you screen record a DM?**
