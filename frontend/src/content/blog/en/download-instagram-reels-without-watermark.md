## For Creators: How to Save Your *Own* Reels Without a Watermark

If you're a creator, you have two excellent, built-in options to save your own work in high quality before Instagram can add a watermark.

### Method 1: Save the Draft Before Publishing

The best practice is to save your video *after* editing but *before* posting.

1.  Create and edit your Reel as usual within the Instagram app.
2.  On the final editing screen (before adding a caption), tap the **Download icon** (an arrow pointing down) at the top of the screen.
3.  This saves a high-resolution, clean copy of your video with all edits and audio directly to your camera roll.

### Method 2: The "Remix" Trick (For Already-Posted Reels)

If you forgot to save your draft and already posted the Reel, you can use this clever workaround discovered by users on Reddit.

1.  Go to the Reel you've already posted on your profile.
2.  Tap the **three dots (...)** to open the menu.
3.  Select **"Remix."**
4.  This opens your Reel in the editor. **Do not make any changes.**
5.  Simply tap the **Download icon** at the top of the screen.

This trick forces Instagram to save a clean version of your video to your device. Note: This only works if you have enabled remixing on your videos.

## Safety Guide: How to Spot a Risky Downloader App

The demand for downloading Reels has led to a flood of unsafe third-party apps. Follow these rules to protect your account:

-   **NEVER Enter Your Login Details:** A legitimate downloader only needs the public URL of the Reel. If any app or website asks for your Instagram username and password, it is a phishing scam designed to steal your account.
-   **Beware of "Private Profile" Downloaders:** No ethical tool can bypass Instagram's privacy settings. Any service claiming to download from private accounts is almost certainly a scam.
-   **Check App Permissions:** If you install a mobile app, check what it wants to access. A video downloader should not need access to your contacts, microphone, or location.
-   **Stick to Browser-Based Tools:** The safest option is a web tool like ours that requires no installation, minimizing the risk of malware or spyware.
