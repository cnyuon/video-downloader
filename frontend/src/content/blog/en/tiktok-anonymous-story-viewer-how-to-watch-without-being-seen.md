Turning off Profile Views only stops people from seeing that you visited their main profile page. It has zero effect on stories. Creators can always see exactly who viewed their story for the full 24 hours it is live.

## Comparing Native Workarounds for Private Accounts: Ghost Account vs. Airplane Mode

### Analysis/Pros & Cons of the 'Airplane Mode' Trick
If the target account is private, web tools will fail. The most widely discussed manual bypass is the Airplane Mode trick. Here is how to execute it:

1. Open TikTok and let the target's profile load (do not tap the story ring yet).
2. Swipe down your phone's control center and turn on Airplane Mode (ensure Wi-Fi is completely off).
3. Tap the profile picture and view the pre-loaded story.
4. Force close the TikTok app immediately.
5. Go to your phone's settings and clear the TikTok app cache before turning Airplane Mode back on.

* **Pros:** Requires no third-party tools or secondary accounts.
* **Cons:** Highly prone to user error. If the cache isn't cleared properly or the app reconnects to the internet a second too soon, your view will register on their list.
